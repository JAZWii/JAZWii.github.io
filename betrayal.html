Betrayal at House on the Hill - The Board Game

A Demo Backend of the famous board game called Betrayal at House on the Hill, developed from scratch using Java and hashcode design pattern. The Game consists of 2 Phases which are Phase 1 that before the Hunt has been Revealed and Phase 2 that after the Hunt has been Revealed.
Phase 1 is 90% completed , overall %60 of the backend has been finished.
