SpaceShip 2D

Spaceship is a 2D platform game developed from scratch by following Course from Udemy by Ben Tristem, Rick Davidson, GameDev.tv Team in addition to my own touches. Several levels were added and 2 different ways of playing were created. The Development was done using Unity 2018.1.1
